About Us

KR SPEED SKATING ACADEMY

KR Speed Skating Academy, located in the heart of VGP Selva Nagar, Velachery, Chennai, is a premier destination for speed skating enthusiasts. Founded with a passion for the sport, the academy is dedicated to nurturing young talent and building champions from the ground up. Our experienced coaches bring discipline, technique, and energy to every session, making skating both fun and competitive.

Whether you are a complete beginner looking to find your footing on skates or an advanced skater aiming to compete at regional and national levels, KR Speed Skating Academy has a program tailored just for you. We offer flexible training schedules, professional guidance, and a safe, encouraging environment where every skater can reach their full potential. Join us and be part of a growing community of speed skating champions in Chennai.

  • Open 7 days a week with flexible training hours to suit every schedule
  • Experienced coaches with competitive skating backgrounds and proven track records
  • Comprehensive programs for all age groups from beginners to national-level competitors
